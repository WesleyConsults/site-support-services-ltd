# Site Support Services Limited — Company Reference Report

## 1. Company Overview

**Site Support Services Limited** is a Ghana-based support services company located in Takoradi. The company provides practical business, operational, and field support services to organizations working in demanding industries such as **Oil & Gas, Construction, Hospitality, Mining, Logistics, and Industrial Operations**.

From the company materials, Site Support Services presents itself as a reliable partner that helps businesses manage the difficult “support” side of operations. This includes catering for workers, supplying manpower, managing facilities and camps, handling logistics, renting equipment and vehicles, supporting QHSE needs, and assisting with travel and visa processes.

The company’s slogan is:

> **Reliability at hand**

This means the brand wants to be seen as dependable, accessible, professional, and ready to support clients whenever needed.

## 7. What the Company Does in Simple Terms

Site Support Services Limited helps companies run their sites and operations smoothly.

For example, when a company has workers on a construction site, offshore project, mining site, port operation, or remote camp, they may need food, accommodation, transportation, cleaning, safety support, equipment, logistics, and manpower. Site Support Services provides these kinds of services so the client does not have to manage everything alone.

In simple terms, the company is a **one-stop operational support partner**.
